ஆல்பம் 28 பாடல் 4

Psalm 115 – Declaration of trust in the living God who remembers, blesses, and protects His people, in contrast to powerless idols.
Closing Prayer
Lord Jesus Christ,
thank You that You remember us.
Bless us and our children.
Help us trust in You alone.
